# Executive Summary

## Vision

To become Indonesia's largest distributed laundry network by connecting customers, independent laundries, and neighborhood Drop Points into one digital ecosystem.

Rather than competing with existing laundries, the platform empowers them to collaborate, share capacity, and grow together.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to digitize Indonesia's fragmented laundry industry by providing:

- More customers for independent laundries
- Better utilization of washing machine capacity
- Easier access to laundry services for customers
- Additional income opportunities for neighborhood businesses through Drop Points

---

## Problem Statement

Indonesia has thousands of independent laundry businesses.

Most of them face two opposite problems.

### Problem 1

Too many orders.

Laundry owners reject customers because machines are already full.

Revenue is lost.

Customer satisfaction decreases.

---

### Problem 2

Too few orders.

Machines remain idle.

Employees have no work.

Rent and operational costs continue.

---

The industry lacks a system that allows laundries to collaborate and share capacity.

---

# Solution

The platform creates a nationwide Laundry Capacity Network.

Instead of every laundry operating independently,

laundries become connected.

Whenever one laundry experiences overload,

the excess work can be distributed to another verified partner with available capacity.

Customers continue receiving their clothes on time,

while both laundries earn revenue.

# Why This Business Can Win

Indonesia has tens of thousands of independent laundries operating independently.

The market lacks a unified platform that allows these businesses to collaborate rather than compete.

By becoming the operating system that connects laundry businesses, customers, and neighborhood Drop Points, the company creates value for every participant in the ecosystem.

Rather than owning washing machines, stores, or logistics fleets, the company builds a scalable digital network where idle capacity becomes productive, overloaded businesses receive support, customers receive better service, and every successful transaction strengthens the network.

The long-term opportunity is not simply to become another laundry application.

The ambition is to become the digital infrastructure powering Indonesia's laundry industry.
