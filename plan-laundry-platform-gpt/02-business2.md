# Executive Summary

The business is a multi-sided marketplace connecting:

- Customers
- Machine Owners (Laundry Businesses)
- Drop Points

Unlike traditional marketplaces, the platform also enables collaboration between Machine Owners through Capacity Exchange.

The primary business objective during the first two years is to achieve positive cash flow while building a scalable recurring revenue model.

# Cash Flow Philosophy

Early-stage startups fail because they run out of cash.

Therefore, the business prioritizes:

1. Positive cash flow.
2. Sustainable recurring revenue.
3. Controlled operational costs.

Profit is important, but survival comes first.
