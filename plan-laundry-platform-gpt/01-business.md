# Executive Summary

Laundry Capacity Network (LCN) is a platform that connects:

- Customers
- Drop Points (DP)
- Machine Owners (MO)

Unlike existing laundry marketplaces, LCN also enables independent laundries to share excess workload through a Capacity Exchange system.

Instead of competing against laundries, the platform helps laundries collaborate.

The long-term goal is to become the operating system for Indonesia's independent laundry industry.

---

# Vision

Build Indonesia's largest connected laundry ecosystem.

Instead of thousands of independent laundries operating alone, create one collaborative network that benefits everyone.

# Industry Problem

Current laundry businesses usually face one of two problems.

## Problem A

Too many orders.

Effects

- Customers rejected
- Late delivery
- Poor customer satisfaction
- Lost revenue

---

## Problem B

Too few orders.

Effects

- Machines idle
- Employees underutilized
- Fixed costs remain
- Low profitability

---

Today there is no standard platform that allows laundries to share workloads.

The platform introduces a Capacity Exchange.

# Current Assumptions

We currently believe:

- Laundry owners need additional orders more than customers need another laundry app.
- Existing laundries are willing to collaborate if incentives are aligned.
- Drop Points can become a scalable distribution network.
- Capacity Exchange creates stronger network effects than a standard marketplace.
- The platform should own the network, not the washing machines.
