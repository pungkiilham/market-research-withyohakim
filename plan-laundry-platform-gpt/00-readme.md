# Overview

Laundry Capacity Network (LCN) is a technology platform that connects customers, independent laundry businesses, and neighborhood Drop Points into one collaborative ecosystem.

Unlike traditional online laundry services that simply connect customers to one laundry shop, LCN enables laundries to share unused washing capacity, helping businesses increase revenue while improving customer experience.

Our goal is **not to replace independent laundries**, but to become the digital infrastructure that helps them grow together.

---

# Vision

Become Indonesia's largest digital infrastructure for the laundry industry by enabling collaboration, capacity sharing, and standardized operations.

---

# Mission

- Help independent laundries earn more revenue.
- Improve utilization of washing machines across Indonesia.
- Make laundry services easier, faster, and more reliable for customers.
- Create new income opportunities for neighborhood businesses through Drop Points.
- Build a trusted and standardized laundry ecosystem.

---

# Core Philosophy

We believe the future of the laundry industry is collaboration instead of competition.

Today, laundries operate independently.

Some receive too many orders.

Some receive too few.

Our platform connects them so excess work can be distributed efficiently, creating value for everyone.

# Platform Roles

## Customer

People who want their clothes washed.

Benefits

- Nearby Drop Points
- Order tracking
- Standardized service
- Faster turnaround

---

## Drop Point (DP)

Neighborhood businesses acting as receiving and pickup locations.

Examples

- Grocery stores
- Convenience stores
- Boarding houses
- Apartment management
- Existing laundries

Benefits

- Additional revenue
- Increased customer traffic
- No washing equipment required

---

## Machine Owner (MO)

Laundry businesses or individuals who own washing equipment.

Benefits

- Additional orders
- Better machine utilization
- Digital business management
- Overflow work from other laundries

---

## Platform

The platform manages

- Order routing
- Capacity matching
- Payments
- Quality standards
- Ratings
- Partner verification
- Marketplace operations

# Business Concept

# Development Principles

Every major decision should follow these principles.

1. Empower independent laundries instead of replacing them.

2. Build trust before pursuing rapid growth.

3. Every feature should provide value to at least two sides of the marketplace.

4. Keep operations as simple as possible.

5. Validate ideas before investing heavily.

6. Prioritize recurring revenue over one-time income.

7. Build network effects that become difficult to replicate.
